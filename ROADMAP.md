# Sarf Atlas Roadmap

Sarf Atlas tracks a paper series around Arabic morphology representation,
probing, evaluation, and intervention work.

## Package Status

- Current PyPI release: `sarf-atlas==0.4.0`.
- Current focus: stabilization, professor packet, and roadmap clarity before
  v0.5 implementation work.

## Engineering Milestones

1. v0.5: optional baseline runners
   ([issue #1](https://github.com/voidwest/sarf-atlas/issues/1)).
2. v0.6: Paper 1-style reproduction example
   ([issue #4](https://github.com/voidwest/sarf-atlas/issues/4)).
3. v0.7: tokenizer-atlas integration or tokenization diagnostics
   ([issue #2](https://github.com/voidwest/sarf-atlas/issues/2)).
4. v1.0: stable CLI and artifact contract
   ([issue #3](https://github.com/voidwest/sarf-atlas/issues/3)).

## Research Arc

1. Paper 1: Leakage-aware probing of Arabic morphology in small language
   models.
2. Paper 2: Scaling, tokenization, and extraction-position replication.
3. Paper 3: Arabic benchmark/evaluation audit.
4. Paper 4: Better evaluation framework for root/pattern/lemma.
5. Paper 5: Representation-guided fine-tuning / intervention.
